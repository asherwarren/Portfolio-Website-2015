A web series, created in partnership with the Johns Hopkins Center for Clinical Global Health Education. Each episode focuses on a single disease, following the stories of the men and women who worked to discover and combat them.

The initial seed of the idea for the series was created by Dr. Bob Bollinger, founding director of the CCGHE. 15Four was given almost total freedom to design what form the series would ultimately take. Primarily a passion project, it’s also meant to act as a tool to increase awareness around the men and women who’ve worked tirelessly over the years to combat disease.

To execute the series, oversight was divided among the 15Four team. My responsibility was to provide overall art direction and production design as well as directing the series title sequence. The opening titles involved extensive preproduction to collect the archival materials and to design scientifically accurate 3D models of the microbes covered in each episode. I personally designed the overall brand for the series, providing art direction that was translated into both the episodes and the series website. I worked with a composer to create a custom musical score and directed photo shoots with our experts to collect still portraits to use for titles as well as on the website.
