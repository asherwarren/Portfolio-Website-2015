A refresh and build out of the 15Four brand and a redesign of the company website.

15Four had gone through a logo redesign several years previously but hadn’t develop any brand guidelines or consistent imagery. The refresh of the brand began with the two elements that had to remain: the number mark and the color cyan. The task was to take these elements and to create the guidelines required to use them effectively and design any elements needed to support them.

I personally wrote the new guidelines; incorporating a broader color palette and library of imagery. I worked closely with our web team to create style tiles for the site and overall structure and navigation. I photographed a series of objects that could be used through the 15Four brand and website. I also created three illustrations representing each of the companies core competencies: animation, video production, and web design. These illustrations were animated and coded so that a user could move the designs in 3D space.
