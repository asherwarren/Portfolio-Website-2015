Live action video brand guidelines to be used across the entire T. Rowe Price organization, laying out how the brand is represented in live action videos and animations as well as providing an extensive motion graphics package.

T. Rowe Price recently completed an extensive brand refresh. 15Four was approached to create the video portion of their new brand guidelines. T. Rowe Price has a robust internal video department and works with several external vendors to support to their extensive video production needs. It was vital to provide guidelines to ensure a consistent look and feel as well as a graphics package that could quickly apply a branded look to every project.

I was given complete oversight of this project, creating the overall structure of the guidelines and then working to design and execute all the materials needed. I personally wrote the guidelines and directed the video and photo shoots to collect needed assets. For the motion graphics package I created the initial designs and worked closely with our animation team to execute and deliver the final assets.
