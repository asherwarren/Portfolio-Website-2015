Branding for Abbott Memorial Presbyterian Church, a historic church located in the heart of Baltimore City.

Abbott Memorial has been continuously operating for over 133 years but has never had a formal logo or visual identity. The new brand needed to reflect both the historic nature of the church as well as its push to remain a modern and active member of their community. The logo design takes the traditional shape of an ornate stamp and strips it down to its most basic pieces. The design balances the historic influence with a clean, simple, inconspicuous approach.
